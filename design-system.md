# Design System : In response to the first part of Issue#1

## Light

BACKGROUND
- primary: white → cyan-50 (gradient)
- secondary: white
- accent: sky-500 → cyan-500 (gradient)

BORDER
- default: gray-200
- subtle-brand: cyan-100 (optional)

FOCUS
- primary: sky-500
- offset: white

FOREGROUND
- primary text: gray-900
- secondary text: gray-600
- muted text: gray-500
- on-accent: white / sky-100
