# BayOfBengal Project

BayOfBengal is a B2B marketplace connecting manufacturers, traders, and suppliers from Bangladesh and its neighboring regions with international buyers. The platform enables global buyers to source from Bengal and its landlocked neighboring regions with confidence through secure and seamless procurement.
